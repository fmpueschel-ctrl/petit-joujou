# Fire400 Design Concepts

<text>
<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Brutalist Luxury / Dark Mode Industrial
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Unapologetic Boldness**: Massive typography, high contrast, no fluff.
  2. **Raw Materiality**: Textures of charred wood, slate, fire, and red wine.
  3. **Ritualistic Flow**: The user journey mimics the dining ritual – strict, guided, exclusive.
  4. **Polarizing Clarity**: "Passt nicht? Move on." translates to visual design – clear cuts, no soft edges.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Background**: Deepest Charcoal (#0a0a0a) – representing the charred crust of the steak and the dark cellar.
  - **Primary Accent**: Ember Red (#ff3300) – vibrant, dangerous, alive. Used for "Fire" elements and CTAs.
  - **Secondary Accent**: Bone White (#f0f0f0) – stark contrast for typography, representing the Tomahawk bone.
  - **Tertiary**: Wine Purple (#4a0e16) – deep, rich, almost black, used for the "400" section backgrounds.
  *Intent*: To evoke the primal nature of fire and meat, and the sophisticated depth of red wine.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Split Screen Dominance**: The homepage is a battleground between "Fire" (Left) and "400" (Right).
  - **Asymmetric Grids**: Content blocks that break the grid, creating tension and energy.
  - **Sticky Typography**: Headlines that stay fixed while imagery scrolls behind them (Parallax).

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **The "Cut" Line**: A sharp, diagonal line separating sections, mimicking a knife cut.
  - **Grain & Noise**: Film grain overlay to give a raw, analog feel.
  - **Floating Bone**: A 3D-like visual of the Tomahawk bone that acts as a navigational anchor.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Hover to Ignite**: Elements glow or spark when hovered.
  - **Forceful Transitions**: Page transitions are instant and sharp, like a knife chop.
  - **Scroll-Triggered Reveal**: Content is hidden in darkness and revealed by "light" (scroll position).

  **Animation**:
  - **Flicker**: Subtle intensity changes in red accents, breathing like embers.
  - **Slide & Slam**: Text enters with weight and impact.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headline**: "Oswald" or "Anton" (Google Fonts) – Tall, condensed, imposing. Uppercase only.
  - **Body**: "Space Grotesk" or "Syne" – Technical, slightly quirky, modern.
</idea>

  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Dramatic Lighting**: Everything looks like it's lit by a single spotlight in a dark room.

  4. **Elegant Restraint**: Less is more. Only the essential elements remain.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Palette**: Monochromatic Black & White with a single "Blood Red" accent.
  - *Intent*: To focus entirely on the texture of the meat and the liquid of the wine. High drama.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Cinematic Full-Screen**: Each section fills the viewport completely.
  - **Center-Stage Hero**: The product (Steak/Wine) is always the absolute center of attention.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Smoke Overlays**: Subtle video loops of smoke drifting over content.
  - **Spotlight Cursor**: The user's cursor acts as a light source, revealing details in the dark.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Slow Burn**: Interactions are smooth, slow, and deliberate.
  - **Hold to Confirm**: Actions require a "long press" to simulate commitment.

  **Animation**:
  - **Fade & Drift**: Elements float into existence.
  - **Cinematic Zoom**: Slow zooms on background imagery.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headline**: "Playfair Display" (Italic) – Elegant, sharp, dangerous.
  - **Body**: "Lato" – Clean, invisible, supportive.

  4. **Loud & Proud**: Screaming for attention.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Palette**: Black, White, Neon Red, and Hot Pink.
  - *Intent*: To signal "Party" and "Eskalation".

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Magazine Collage**: Text and images scattered like a punk zine.
  - **No Grid**: Elements placed "randomly" (but carefully balanced).

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Tape & Stickers**: Visual elements that look like tape holding things together.
  - **Graffiti Scrawls**: Hand-drawn arrows and circles highlighting key info.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Glitch Effects**: Elements distort on interaction.
  - **Rapid Fire**: Fast, snappy animations.

  **Animation**:
  - **Shake & Jitter**: Elements vibrate with energy.
  - **Strobe**: Flashing effects (used carefully).

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headline**: "Permanent Marker" or "Rock Salt" – Handwritten, aggressive.
  - **Body**: "Courier Prime" – Typewriter style, raw data.
